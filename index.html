Here is how to play

×

The game has one simple goal:
collect all the coins.

LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys are to move around.

Don't let the lava touch you or the level restarts!

Enemies will take a live and restart a level when they touch you.

Land on top of the enemy to destroy them!

UP key is to jump.

Have fun jumping around. ^_^
